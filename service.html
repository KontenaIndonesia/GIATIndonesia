Service coverage

Before subscribing to our service, make sure that:

Can your design brief be explained via email?

If so, then it sounds joyful. However, if you need a phone call, meeting, or even detailed guide text, just to explain the design brief, then GIAT is not the right place for you.

GIAT is not a complex production house of graphic design, but rather a simple workshop for designers.

Therefore, we have some limitations:

First - GIAT is not a replacement for your in-house designer right now. We provide complementary designers to perfect your graphic design needs.

Secondly - GIAT will allocate 1 designer for each project from you (Standard package). And, provide 3 designers per project for the Dayshift package. We are willing to work on any project from agency, consultant, to individuals who need graphic design.

In order to avoid any misunderstanding in the process, please remember that our designers are human so the project can not be completed in one blink. For the usual speed of workmanship, please choose Standard Pack with long workmanship per design maximum 3 business day. As for the work of 1x24 hours business day, select Dayshift Pack.
