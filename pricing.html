    Our hot questions

    What is the 14-Day No-Risk Money Back Guarantee?

    Each client will get 14-Day No-Risk protection, where you are entitled to refund 100% of the payment amount already paid.

    What graphic design can work with?

    GIATTT dotcom is willing to work on all graphic design requests. We also do not want to do UI / UX design, original logos or branding, video, or complex illustrations. Read scope.

    Unlimited requests? Unlimited revisions?

    Yep! You are entitled to submit as many graphic design requests and revisions as possible. However, do not forget. Our designers are human so the work is not one touch.

    How long until the demand can begin to work?

    After submitting your request, you need to settle the billing of the selected package. Furthermore, 1 to 2 business day graphic design requests will first be completed by our team.

    How quickly is the request completed?

    For Standard package services, the request will be completed for a maximum of 3 business days. As for the package Dayshift, the work will be faster that is a maximum of 1x24 business day.

    I need 1000 designs per week

    Standard Package only provides 1 designer per project. While the package Dayshift, 2-3 designers working on a project so that the work is much faster.
